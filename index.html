Our Story

Best Friends.
Big Dreams.
Loud Lemons.

Best friends since 3rd grade. A shared love for mid-2000s emo and punk rock. Seven babies between them. Yeah, life is loud, and they like it that way.

Dani and Misti launched The Sugar Cube in April 2026 with a converted horse trailer, a squeeze of rebellion, and more flavor than any lemonade stand has a right to have.

Fresh squeezed, a little (probably a lot) chaotic, and way more fun than it should be. You'll find them wherever the music is loud and the lemons are squeezing.
